There are two investment banks when preparing for the back half of a life — financial and emotional. Did you build the relationships you needed to make the second half fulfilling? Compound interest works the same way with people as it does with money. Neglecting your children's emotional development is the equivalent of skipping a 401k contribution. The framework is precise, load-bearing, and almost nobody has named it. The opening image is a parent whose account was empty when the family needed to withdraw. The footnotes are a literal ledger — deposits and withdrawals catalogued like transactions.
